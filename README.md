# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard — Looker Studio

An interactive 4-page business intelligence dashboard built in Google Looker Studio using a custom e-commerce dataset.

## 🔗 Live Dashboard
[View Dashboard](https://datastudio.google.com/s/i4cMgyrXtN0)

## 📋 Pages
- **Page 1 – Executive Summary**: Revenue, Profit, Margin scorecards, monthly trend table, revenue by region and customer segment
- **Page 2 – Sales Performance**: Revenue by category, sales manager leaderboard, quarterly pivot table, revenue by month
- **Page 3 – Marketing & Channel Analysis**: Sessions by channel, conversion rate, bounce rate vs session duration, revenue by channel
- **Page 4 – Operations & Customer Experience**: CSAT & NPS scores, order status distribution, returns by category, revenue by platform

## 📁 Dataset
- 2,500 rows | 28 columns | Jan 2023 – Dec 2024
- Fields: Revenue, Profit, Region, Category, Channel, Platform, Customer Segment, CSAT, NPS and more
